Theoretical Framework

The Synthesis

PC-98 Dithering: "Here's a constraint (16 colors). We'll work beautifully within it through ordered patterns."

Glitch Feminism: "Even the adaptation can be refused. The pattern itself breaks down."

Visual Result: You see BOTH order (dither) and chaos (glitch) simultaneously—worldtraveling made visible.

Hyper Light Drifter Inspiration

HLD uses chromatic aberration (cyan/magenta separation) on movement and corruption. We apply this to dithering patterns—the constraint workaround itself becomes the site of glitching.

Color Politics: Not RGB

Traditional chromatic aberration uses Red, Green, Blue (technical reality of screens).

We use Magenta, Cyan, Pink, Lime (political color choices):

Magenta

Red + Blue simultaneously. Refuses binary (pink=fem, blue=masc)

Cyan

Blue + Green simultaneously. Between categories

Hot Pink

Riot grrrl, punk feminism. Reclaimed aggressive femininity

Lime Green

"Toxic femininity". Unnatural, refusing organic essentialism
